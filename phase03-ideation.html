Phase 03 · Ideation

Generate many ideas before choosing the best solution.

In this phase, your team uses the HMW question from Define to brainstorm widely, apply SCAMPER, evaluate ideas and justify one final concept before prototyping.

What is Ideation?

Ideation is the creative phase where your team generates many possible solutions. The goal is not to find the perfect idea immediately, but to explore widely before selecting the strongest direction.

What students need to do

  • Use the best HMW question from T06 as the brainstorming focus.
  • Generate at least 20 ideas without judging them too early.
  • Use SCAMPER to improve, combine or transform ideas.
  • Score the top 5 ideas using the Idea Selection Matrix.
  • Justify one final idea and submit Supervisor Gate 2 before Prototyping.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Do not stop after the first "good" idea.
  • Do not criticise ideas during brainstorming.
  • Do not choose based only on personal preference.
  • Do not forget to connect the idea back to the problem statement.

Target20+ ideas

A7Brainstorm Rules

No judging during idea generation.

Build on each other's ideas using "Yes, and…".

Write fast and aim for quantity.

Wild ideas are allowed because they may spark better ideas.

Sample Brainstorm

HMW: Help students decide where to buy lunch before peak-hour rush

Ideas: crowd-level display, pre-order lunch slot, canteen queue camera, quick lunch menu board, student lunch planner, Telegram lunch alert.
✍️ Sample answer guide

HMW: How might we help students decide where to buy lunch before peak-hour rush?
Idea: Show live crowd level for each food stall before students leave class.

Activity Introduction

What are you going to do?

You will examine your top ideas through seven SCAMPER lenses: Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to another use, Eliminate and Reverse.

InputTop ideas from T07
ToolSCAMPER 7 lenses
OutputImproved ideas
Sample SCAMPER

Improve the crowd-level display idea

Combine: combine live crowd level with pre-order lunch slots.
Eliminate: remove complex login for users who only want to check queue level.
✍️ Sample answer guide

Substitute: Replace manual queue checking with student-reported crowd status.
Combine: Combine crowd status with menu availability.
