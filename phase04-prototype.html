Phase 04 · Prototype

Build a simple version to test your idea.

In this phase, your team builds a low-fidelity prototype, records versions, uploads evidence and prepares for user testing in Phase 05.

What is Prototype?

Prototyping is where your team builds a quick, simple version of the final idea. The goal is not perfection — it is making the idea testable so real users can react to something concrete.

What students need to do

  • Plan the prototype type, materials, roles and success criteria using T11.
  • Build and document at least 3 prototype versions in T12.
  • Upload or link prototype evidence in T13.
  • Complete the Prototype Readiness Check before Phase 05.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Do not build the final product. Build just enough to test.
  • Do not skip version documentation. Record every change.
  • Do not use complex tools when paper or Canva works fine.
  • Do not move to testing without evidence of what was built.
